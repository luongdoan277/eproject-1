Latest notice about shipping:
We recently upgraded our delivery system in October 2019 to deliver orders to our customers in a faster and cheaper way.
* For orders with an amount of over VND 1159,000, we will not charge shipping (previously required to be higher than VND 2940,000 for free shipping).
And the delivery time has been reduced to 5-7 working days (previously 5-12 working days). * Saving delivery time has been reduced to 10-12 business days (previously 15-25 working days).
* Estimated shipping time starts from the date of shipment, not the date of order.
It may take longer than expected due to invalid address, clearance, or other reasons.
* If the package does not arrive in time, please submit your information within 6 months when the item is delivered from our warehouse and our agent will respond within 24 hours.
