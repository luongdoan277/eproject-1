RETURN POLICY

Instructions for change and return

We are sorry if you are not 100% satisfied with the items you have received and we are happy to accept returns within 30 days of receiving most of your items.



1. Please send questions to contact customer service about the delivery address, In that question please indicate the goods you want to return and the reason, you will receive a reply within 24 hours. Now, please be patient.
2. Return packaged goods according to the return information provided by customer service
3. Mail the parcel at your local post office.
4. When we receive your package, we will process the exchange or refund to your account on time according to our return terms.

Conditions of return


1. You have 30 days to decide if an item is right for you. If you are not satisfied, please contact us within 30 days after receiving the item.
2. The following items cannot be returned or replaced: bras, lingerie, pajamas, swimwear, jewelry and accessories (except scarves, bags and mermaid blankets).
3. Returned item must be used with original package. We do not accept returned, worn, damaged, washed or altered items of any kind.
4. Branded items are not refundable and free gifts cannot be returned.
5. We do not accept returns that have been sent without contacting us.
6. We do not accept parcels returned by COD service. Return shipping fee is not refundable.
7. Please be sure to double check your returned items before shipping. We will not be responsible for returning non-SHEIN items.
Note : Shipping fees and shipping guarantees do not support refunds.

Return address


If you wish to return the product to the nearest return address, please submit the information in your account and our customer service staff will respond within 24 hours.
Note : Please do not send back to the address on your package. This is not our return address and will affect the processing of your refund.
